What are we about?

Here is three of our main values as a philoslopy.

Collaberative Veto

Scientific self-determination is a collaboration of ideas concretely formulated into one philosophy, an idea that is a collaborative veto. Allowing local levels and individuals the ability to "say no" is a fundamental part of consent of the governed.

Collaberative veto

Multiple Choice Voting

Multiple choice voting is another idea which Scientific self-determination has adopted. The ability for each voter to have a vote for every candidate on the ballot.

Multiple Choice Voting

All Accepting

Scientific self-determination and all parties affiliate with it accept all to vote and participate in certain actions in the party. Meaning you can be a Democrat or Republican and still vote in the SSD party.
