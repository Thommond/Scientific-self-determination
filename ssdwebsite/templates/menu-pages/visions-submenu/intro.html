Issues

We Believe that alot of political issues come from two main points.

  1. Not having consent of the governed
  2. Being to broad so individuals seem powerless

Collaberate Veto

Consent of the governed through specific form voting.

The average person can have their entire way of life destroyed from a misguided, politically motivated decision by those who do not understand or live in the local community, and the community at large. The way to reduce this issue is allow local communities to have a larger impact. If we allow local communities to "say no" to a broader governments law, power or action, this will reduce unwanted local changes.

All Accepting

Continuous learning, we except independents and other party members.

Instablility

The current political system and structure is unsustainable, any political structure which is unsustainable will disable positive elements of society while enabling negative ones. It does not matter if they are rich, poor, majority, minority, religous, non-religous or what race, gender, sexuality and if you're foriegn or domestic.

Finding The Good

We believe in embolding and finding the good in everyone. We feel under the curretn system as it is, no ome is being satisfied and the costs are not being paid by those making the bad decesions.

Multiple Choice Voting

The ability to vote for who, you want when you want.

Multiple choice voting would allow you to choose as many candidate in the election that you think would be reasonable to perform the duties of the office for which they are running.

Why should you be limited to having your opinion for just one candidate? Multiple choice voting would work the same way as our current voting system; the candidate with the most votes wins.
